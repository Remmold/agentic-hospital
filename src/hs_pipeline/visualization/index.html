About This Simulation

This system simulates a hospital workflow, where AI agents (nurses, doctors, lab techs) collaborate to diagnose patients step by step.

How it works: Each case begins with triage, followed by a doctor’s exam, then lab tests. Agents learn from outcomes to improve diagnoses.

Your role: Watch as agents work through each scenario. The right panel shows the decision timeline.
